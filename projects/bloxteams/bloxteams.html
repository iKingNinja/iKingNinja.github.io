The BloxTeams Platform

BloxTeams is not my personal project but rather a team project of which I am a co-founder and it is developed at bloxteams.com.


Developing BloxTeams taught and still teaches me a lot; it helped me improve my programming skills, in fact BloxTeams was near release on June/July 2022 but looking at the code I realized it was terribly inefficient and which all the new things and concepts I had learned it could've been improved a lot hence I decided to rewrite the entire back-end.


The story

BloxTeams was born from the idea me and Paige had back in 2021 to provide a free and accessible platform to all Roblox developers who were afraid of engaging in commissions due to scammers being always behind the corner. Works started around May 2022 when the first commit was made.
